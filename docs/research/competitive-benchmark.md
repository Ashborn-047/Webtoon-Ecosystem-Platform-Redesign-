## Best Practices Identified

### 1. Personalization
- **Tapas**: Advanced recommendation engine with "Because you read..." prompts
- **Implementation**: Match percentages and genre tags

### 2. Creator Engagement
- **Tapas**: Prominent creator activity feeds
- **Implementation**: Horizontal scrolling creator cards with follow buttons

### 3. Visual Hierarchy
- **Lezhin**: Clear progress indicators for ongoing series
- **Implementation**: Progress bars with episode counts

## Differentiation Opportunities

1. **Enhanced Canvas Visibility**: Unique "Indie Spotlight" framing
2. **Behavioral Design**: Zeigarnik Effect implementation
3. **Editorial Curation**: "Staff Pick" badges for quality signals

## Recommendations

- Adopt personalization patterns from Tapas
- Enhance creator visibility beyond current implementation
- Implement progress tracking similar to Lezhin
