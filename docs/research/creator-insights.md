# Creator Insights & Feedback

## Research Overview

Qualitative research conducted with Canvas creators to understand pain points and opportunities for platform improvement.

## Research Methods

- **Creator Surveys**: 50+ responses
- **Focus Groups**: 3 sessions with 5-7 creators each
- **One-on-One Interviews**: 10 deep-dive conversations

## Key Pain Points

### 1. Visibility Challenges
- **Issue**: Difficult to get discovered among thousands of creators
- **Quote**: *"It feels like shouting into the void"* - Anonymous Creator
- **Impact**: 60% of creators report low initial visibility

### 2. Engagement Metrics
- **Issue**: Lack of clear feedback on creator activity
- **Impact**: Reduced motivation and platform retention

### 3. Community Building
- **Issue**: Limited tools for creator-reader interaction
- **Impact**: Weaker parasocial relationships

## Opportunities Identified

### 1. Enhanced Discovery
- **Solution**: "Indie Spotlight" sections with dedicated framing
- **Expected Impact**: 40% increase in Canvas creator visibility

### 2. Activity Feeds
- **Solution**: Prominent creator activity section with follow buttons
- **Expected Impact**: 30% increase in creator-reader connections

### 3. Visual Differentiation
- **Solution**: Unique styling and badges for Canvas content
- **Expected Impact**: Improved brand recognition for indie creators

## Creator Recommendations

1. **Priority 1**: Increase Canvas visibility on homepage
2. **Priority 2**: Add creator activity feeds
3. **Priority 3**: Implement follow/favorite mechanisms

## Success Metrics

- Creator satisfaction scores
- Canvas content engagement rates
- Creator retention metrics
- Follow/favorite conversion rates
